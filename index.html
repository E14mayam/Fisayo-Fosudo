About Me

I am from Epe, Lagos State, and was born into an amazing family with three younger siblings. I have been interested in media and technology since I was 17 years old but did not have the tools at that age. Neither did I have money to fund any successful venture. It started from drawing comic books in primary school, then learning and thoroughly practising graphic design. I pursued a freelance design career, and took the proceeds to get started in video production for mobile technology reviews. Today, I have founded a media company and I’m grateful to have produced video contents which have been viewed 11.8 million times on YouTube in the past four years. I have also worked with over 70 technology, finance and related brands locally and internationally. In January 2021, I started to explore more economical and financial aspects of Nigeria and this morphed into a ‘Finance Friday’ series which had been viewed 1.2 million times as of August 2021.
